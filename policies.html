Sant Seva Ride Cabs - Privacy Policy

At Sant Seva Ride Cabs, we value your trust and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you share with us when using our services. By booking with us or using our services, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

We collect personal details such as your name, phone number, email address, and pickup/drop-off locations to provide seamless and personalized ride services. If you book a ride online, we may also collect payment details to process your transactions securely. Additionally, we collect information automatically, such as your device details, browser type, and location, to enhance your experience and optimize our services.

We use your information to confirm bookings, provide customer support, improve our services, send ride updates, and offer promotions tailored to your preferences. Your data is shared only with trusted third parties, such as payment gateways and legal authorities, when necessary to comply with regulations or protect our rights.

We use cookies and similar technologies to track website usage, improve functionality, and provide relevant content. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings, although some features may be limited. Your privacy is important to us, and you have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. You can also opt out of receiving marketing communications by following the unsubscribe options provided in our emails or by contacting us directly.

We ensure your information is secure with encryption, restricted access, and regular security measures. While your data may be stored or processed in different countries where we operate, we adhere to strict security standards to protect your information.

This policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any significant updates will be shared on our website. For any questions or concerns about your privacy, please contact us at santsevarides@gmail.com.

Thank you for choosing Sant Seva Ride Cabs. Your trust and privacy are our priorities, and we are committed to delivering safe and reliable services.
